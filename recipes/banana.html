Ham Banana Gratin

Description

This is a very simple recipe with ham, bananas and cream.

Baked in the oven, served with some rice, it's a great meal, inexpensive and easily made.

Ingredients

  • 8 bananas
  • 1 tsp curry
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • 2 dl of cream

How to prepare

  1. Spread ham with mustard
  2. Season with curry and wrap bananas
  3. Lightly grease the gratin dish with butter
  4. Fill in the ham bananas
  5. Bake on the 2nd groove (250Â°C/20min)
