Ham Banana Gratin

Description

This is a very simple recipe with ham, bananas and cream.

Baked in the oven, served with some rice, it's a great meal, inexpensive and easily made.

Ingredients

  • 8 bananas
  • 1 tsp curry
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • 2 dl of cream

How to prepare

  1. Pat salmon filets dry and season with spices on all sides
  2. Heat butter and oil, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice
  3. Add salmon filets and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Use a spoon to baste the filets
  5. Broil for 5 - 6 minutes, until salmon is sticky sweet
