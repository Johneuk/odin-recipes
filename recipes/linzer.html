Linz Cake

Description

Linzer torte is known worldwide as a Viennese pastry, although it is quite common in Germany and neighboring countries.

It is made with a buttery pastry dough with a significant amount of ground nuts, typically hazelnuts, but often with almonds or walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 9 oz butter softened (½ lb + 2 tbsp)
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 ½ tablespoon vanilla sugar or extract

How to prepare

  1. Using the paddle attachment, cream the softened butter and sugar in a stand mixer, at a medium speed
  2. Add the two egg whites and one egg yolk and continue to mix until fluffy. Next add vanilla, cinnamon, almond extract, ground cloves, salt, baking powder and mix it
  3. Then add finely ground almonds and mix that in
  4. Next, add the flour in two installments and mix it in. Increase the speed and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. The dough can also be made by hand
  5. Divide the dough into two portions. One you can use for the classic Linzer torte. The other for the fancy version
