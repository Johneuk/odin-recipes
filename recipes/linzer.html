Linz Cake

Description

Linzer torte is known worldwide as a Viennese pastry, although it is quite common in Germany and neighboring countries.

It is made with a buttery pastry dough with a significant amount of ground nuts, typically hazelnuts, but often with almonds or walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning

How to prepare

  1. Pat salmon filets dry and season with spices on all sides
  2. Heat butter and oil, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice
  3. Add salmon filets and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Use a spoon to baste the filets
  5. Broil for 5 - 6 minutes, until salmon is sticky sweet
