Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

Description

After one bite of this ultra tender salmon, you will absolutely love salmon.

I literally did a little dance in the kitchen because I finally found a way people can enjoy salmon.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning

How to prepare:

  1. Pat salmon filets dry and season with spices on all sides
  2. Heat butter and oil, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice
  3. Add salmon filets and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Use a spoon to baste the filets
  5. Broil for 5 - 6 minutes, until salmon is sticky sweet
  6. Enjoy your meal!
